Concentrates:

Small glass viles that when consumed give you unique buffs based on the sin until the next dawn. As an action you may consume a vile. After the vile is consumed, the user may transition in and out of a radiant form (action) giving them new abilities that do not interfere with their normal abilities unless specified. See below for specific abilities.

Concentrated Pride

Effects last 10 minutes

While in pride's radiant form, you gain a radiant aura, all creatures within 30 ft radius of you must succeed on a WIS 20 saving throw or be charmed by you. While charmed, the entity will complete small tasks for you at your request and will remain on good terms with you until you transform out of the radiant form. The tasks given cannot be too complex or inflict suffering on any party, moreover if the target leaves your radius the effects dissipate.

Gift of song -

As an action, you may grace an entity of your choice with a blessing. The entity will automatically succeed any persuasion roll that has a check of 19 or lower. This effect lasts until you determine otherwise or until you shift out of your radiant form.

Concentrated Greed

Effects last 10 minutes

While in greed's radiant form, entities around you become infatuated with the trinkets and treasures you create. You will constantly be generating illusory treasure while in this form to do with what you please.

Greed's Fortune -

With this illusory treasure, you may offer as a trade to any entity you wish as an action. If the entity is not already willing to trade, they must succeed on a INT 20 saving throw or be forced to take the deal you offer. Only one deal may take place at once, however once a deal has been formed, you may offer more treasure in exchange for more service from the creature at a reduced or null INT saving throw.
