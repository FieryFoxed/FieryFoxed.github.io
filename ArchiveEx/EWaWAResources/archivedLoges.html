Log 1:

–Eggman's Chambers

Subjects Jeremy and REDACTED find themselves responsible for the overseeing of the archmage Eggman. After making their way to the archmages chambers, they find the mage asleep, and seize the opportunity to explore the surrounding chambers. Jeremy finds a magic wand,while REDACTED locates a book to identify the wand. However before REDACTED has the opportunity to inspect the book, Eggman wakes from his slumber. General care routine starts, as Eggman begins eating eggshells and playing with toys strewn across the floor. Jeremy and REDACTED take their time to be gentle, walking on eggshells (literally), in an attempt to not provoke the archmage. Unfortunately, Eggman loses his composure, at which point Eggman casts a spell at random and a white light engulfs the room.

–-Southern Demon Isles

Jeremy and REDACTED find themselves in a combination of planes and wooded landscapes surrounded by farmland. Nearby they spot a small farming house, and with their newfound purpose of attempting to locate Eggman, head towards it in search of clues. There they meet up with a farmer, who tips them in on a few details on which direction the archmage might be heading, but more importantly the direction of the next town. Assuming that Eggman would most likely head towards civilization, the two set off down the dirt path that pushes through the wheatfields. However at the end of the wheat fields, as the path deviates by the forest, the two spot eggman heading into the woods.

Log 2:

–Woods on the Southern Demon Isles

Jeremy and REDACTED set chase attempting to catch up to Eggman, to which they catch up to them after a brief run through the forest. They take a moment to try to calm down Eggman, however at that very moment two stone giants wander out from a displaced dungeon underneath a cave face nearby. The three engage the creatures in combat. While Jeremy’s and REDACTED’s attacks seem less than effective on the creature, Eggman's random use of magic yields varying effects, coating the area in a thick green slime, summoning dead giants, granting trees life and limbs, and summoning one of Energy’s angels. With this new entourage the group easily dispatches the stone giant.

In a bout of desperation at the chaotic and destructive nature of eggman, Jermey asks the angel if it can use its divine power to make Eggman less volatile. The angel agrees in exchange for it being able to return to its original realm. The angel uses its ability to restore Eggman's Wisdom and Intelligence from 0 to their original stats, then quickly departs. Eggman is stunned for a moment as he realizes he is freed from his self imprisonment. Eggman determines that since he will never be able to fully imprison himself, and that he is unwilling to kill himself, that he will return to his original desire of world domination. As a gesture of goodwill to Jeremy for helping him attain this revelation and in exchange for the wand, he offers Jeremy one use of his wish spell.

Log 3:

–Woods on the Southern Demon Isles

Jeremy, who is in patronage to a dark entity at the cost of immense suffering, seized this opportunity using the spell to wish for his own power. The wish spell, in turn, sets Jeremy to the status of godhood. After this concludes, Eggman departs, leaving Jeremy and REDACTED alone in the wooded clearing. The two take this time to read the book on the wand, revealing the wand to be the only artifact known by mortals to possess power rivaling that of Eggmans. This artifact comes at a cost however, often destroying itself and the user when its power is matched. Later in the book it also reveals that something created with Eggman's power or the wand's power, can be refined by divine forces on divine ground to replicate the artifact. The two, assuming that Eggman will become a problem in the future, make it their goal to replicate the wand to end the archmage, immediately setting upon the magical living trees that eggman accidentally created during the stone giant fight. They collect 8 logs from the creature, then set their sights on finding holy lands.

While the two are attempting to determine their next course of action, a small party of adventures come across them. Gavin Elmose the Paladin, Saveth the Monk, Da’rak the Ranger, and Jill the Clerik. Conversation breaks out between the small group, after a brief conversation, the party agrees to lead the two back to Sharlon stride. On the journey back to town, Jeremy attempts to convince Gavin to assist them in their quest to end Eggman. Gavin is hesitant right off the bat, but says he will ask his party members their opinion and get back to him on the proposal.

Log 8:

–Demon Seas

The crew takes a couple weeks on the seas adjusting to the lifestyle. Ruby takes time distributing and documenting rations, setting up schedules, and teaching the mercenaries how to navigate. Jeremy takes time attempting to bleed the ideas of his religion and ideology into the minds of the crew, there are a few members that bite and slowly over time they begin to believe his teachings. During this time Ruby discovers that the goods they picked up from the cleric are filled with valuable materials, magic items, weapons, and a slew of other valuables. They determine once they get to the mainland they will work to obtain a full map of the world and sell off the obtained goods.

After a couple weeks, the crew locates a small island off in the distance. After some brief debating the crew decides to dock and take time to explore the island. Ruby and Jeremy more specifically take a small rowboat driven by the mercenaries, and begin to explore the beach. After finding nothing of interest, they decide to head into the jungle segment of the island. After exploring longer they hear a whispering voice telling them to get down and hide. Heading the warning the two jump into the bushes, finding the voices to be a disheveled man and woman warning them to be as still and quiet as possible. After waiting a few seconds, a sphynx wanders through the foliage in front of them. After the beast leaves the man and woman introduce themselves and explain the island itself is a massive titan that sinks ships that get too close, they were in this situation but barely managed to get to island, dodging the resident sphynx and foraging for about a week. Jeremy and Ruby explain that they have a ship just off shore and determine that it's just a matter of time before the island comes to life and destroys it. After determining the coast was clear, the four make a break for the shore, and after breaking through the tree line find the sphynx also breaking through the leaves after them. Ruby commands the other three to get to the row boat while she holds off the sphinx, engaging it in combat and using magic to impede its progress. After slowing the beast, Ruby uses magic and pure strength to propel herself towards the row boat. After making it a decent distance from the shoreline the sphinx continues its ravenous pursuit, while at the same time the island begins to rise from the water, creating a massive sinking hole of sand and water enderneigh it. As the island reveals itself to be a giant titan crab, the emergence of rushing water impedes both the rowboats' movement and Ruby’s progress. Before Ruby can sink into the watery void, she manages to propel herself out again with the assistance of Jeremy’s magic, returning to the row boat. The members of the rowboat do their best to move against the rushing water, avoid the massive slamming claws from the titan slamming all around them, and impede the movement of the sphinx. Ruby commands the Santiana’s crew to get the boat moving, before attempting to freeze the sphinx in the water. This maneuver is successful, but right as the spell is completed the massive claw of the crab slams on the tiny row boat, sending its contents flying everywhere. Ruby sinks with her anchor, coaxing the sphinx to chase her under the water, while using magic to assist the other members of the rowboat back to the ship. With the assistance of Jeremy, the crew of the Santiana, and Ruby’s magic they manage to successfully get everyone back on board. Ruby uses more spells to propel herself back on board the ship leaving the crab in their dust. While on board the sphinx manages to catch up, but is quickly dispatched by the crew.

–Southern Demon Capital

The Santiana, spotting a modest size city on the shore harboring multiple docks, finds a vacant spot and begins tying up their vessel. Almost immediately, they are stopped by an official who attempts to ascertain who they are and what they are doing without proper documentation. The man reveals himself to be the port master's assistant, to which after a brief conversation Santiago convinces the man that he is a friend of the port master and is here to greet his old friend, however more importantly that they were here to sell crates of goods. The portmasters assistant tells the crew to wait at their ship, while he goes off to confirm their story and set up a meeting time with the portmaster. The four members wait patiently by their vessel and after a brief time the portmasters assistant returns with a handful of guards, the portmasters assistant tells them that the portmaster was interested in setting up a meeting right away, and that they sent out guards to collect their cargo and bring it to the meeting so that they can discuss selling it right away. Following the portmasters assistant through the dock, town area, and over to the postmaster's office they notice that the area around the office building is covered in barracks and more importantly that a giant statue of the cleric they took the goods from was sitting right next to the path they were taking to the office in an outside corridor. The group is escorted to a waiting room briefly before being let into the postmaster's office. After entering, Santiago immediately jumps into action greeting the portmaster like an old friend, which surprisingly is reciprocated by the portmaster. This goes on for a few minutes while Santiago struggles to keep up the facade, before the portmaster reveals that his friend Santiago was the one responsible for taking out his left eye, and subsequently banished from the southern demon isles. The facade crumbling, Santiago comes clean about the situation, the portmaster gets straight to the point after this event. He explains to the party that the goods they are trying to sell have been identified as the contents of a resupply mission personally taken on by one of their best generals Dogsna. The portmaster explains that they are seizing the cargo, the ship, and will be arresting the party unless they repay their aggression. The demands are simple, the Santiana is required to reobtain Dogsna and transport her along with the cargo to the northern demon isles. The group agrees in exchange for their freedom, the privilege of using some of the magic items they stole from Dogsna, and a map of the world. The group leaves the office with a new mission in hand, find Dogsna.

The crew takes some time to themselves, Jeremy heads out with the children and Sammy the maid to go explore the shops, Santiago performs on a street corner and then returns a dementia patient back to their home, Ruby sets out trying to find a method to locate Dogsna, and Dall winds up perusing an underground beast ring.

Ruby finds herself in the Magic Emporium and speaks to the imp that runs the establishment. When explaining that what she's looking for is some type of scrying device, the imp leads her into the back room. When walking into the back room it's revealed to be a massive factory/warehouse covered head to toe in imps and magic items. She is led over to a scrying eye which she purchases from the owner in exchange for her completing a task for him. The owner gives her a small pouch full of crystals and asks her to put them into an empty building in a new town. Agreeing to these terms she leaves with the scrying eye and crystals in hand.

Dall makes his way to a sketchy alleyway, to which he knocks on a door hidden in the wall. After a brief conversation with the bouncer he is let into a dark underground tunnel. At the end he comes to a massive underground cavern full of cages containing mythical creatures and beasts. The owner leads him through different cages making sales pitched the entire time. After he comes to a magical cage with a floating blue cat, he knocks the owner unconscious and steals his key, unlocking the cat's cage and bolting with it. He makes his way back to the ship talking with the magical cat.
